What is Lumina Hackathon?

The Lumina Hackathon brings together the brightest minds in tech to tackle real-world problems, build innovative solutions, and connect with industry experts. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a beginner, this event is your chance to shine!

Who Can Participate?

Open to students, professionals, and anyone passionate about technology and innovation.

Event Schedule

  • Day 1: Registration, Welcome Ceremony, Keynote Speech, Team Formation
  • Day 2: Hacking Sessions, Workshops, Mentorship Hours
  • Day 3: Final Submissions, Presentations, Awards Ceremony
